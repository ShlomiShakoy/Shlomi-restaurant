About

I was born and raised in Tel-Aviv in 1999.
My desire for food cooking started already as a child. My first dish I made when I was 4 years old.
Everything I learned as a child was from my grandmothers that have uniqe kitchen and wonderful dishes.
In the army service, I cooked to my team, and most of them permanently said:"what would we do without you!".That gaves me the desire to continue and persists in my dream! In these days, I am working on my next resturant in Jerusalem.
I hope that you will enjoy this website.
